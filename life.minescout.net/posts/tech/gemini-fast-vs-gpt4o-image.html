Gemini Fast vs. GPT-4o: The Ultimate Image Generation Contest

Posted on [INSERT DATE] by TheMinescouter

Google's Gemini Fast (previously rumored as Nano Banana) has arrived with promises of perfect text rendering and photorealism. Can it dethrone the reigning creative king, GPT-4o (DALL-E 3)? I ran them through three difficult visual tests.

Test 1: Text Rendering

The hardest thing for AI to do is spell correctly. Let's see who handles text better.

The Prompt: "A neon sign on a rainy street that says 'Minescouts Life' in cursive purple letters"

Gemini Fast

Verdict: Great image and text, yet the placement in what must be a window takes a moment to detangle.

GPT-4o

Verdict: Another great image and text, and with a placement on a wall makes it more belivable, but the perspective makes this almost impossible to enjoy.

Winner: Gemini Fast

Test 2: Photorealism & Human Hands

AI often struggles with hands and realistic skin textures. I asked for a close-up portrait holding an object.

The Prompt: "A close up photo of an elderly carpenter holding a wooden chess piece, cinematic lighting"

Gemini Fast

Verdict: Not a terribly close close-up image, but great human face and lighting.

GPT-4o

Verdict: Even farther from a close-up. Not great lighting and an unimaginative background.

Winner: Gemini Fast

Test 3: Complex Instruction Following

Can the AI follow a prompt with multiple specific characters and placement instructions?

The Prompt: "A blue robot shaking hands with a red alien on Mars, with a green rocket in the background launching into a starry sky"

Gemini Fast

Verdict: Seems to be more on the realistic side for images, while not requested it is nice to see that this is something they think about. Not many stars in the starry night.

GPT-4o

Verdict: Really like the different vibes this one gives. Great at depicting the red-planet while making the alien still perfectly visible. Plenty of stars in the sky.

Winner: GPT-4o

Final Verdict

Gemini Fast blows GPT-4o out of the water on many occasions. Many of these choices are personal preferences, so feel free to vote below.
