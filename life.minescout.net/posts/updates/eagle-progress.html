Minescouts Eagle Progress

Originally posted on April 11, 2025 by The Minescout Scouter, Updated 8/23/2025

Welcome to The Minescout Scouter. Here I post my updates on my eagle scout rank, the highest rank of BSA scouting available. Below is a progress bar on what I have completed for this rank.

---

Rank Progress
67%

---

Eagle Project

One of the largest and most time consuming requirements of earning this rank is leading a project.

What is Done?

I have figured out the project, which is creating a little free library. I have also gone out and met with an official from the city, and got the location and idea approved for this project. More Recently, I have constructed the Little Free Libray and emailed the city to plan for installation

What I'm Doing

I have recently gotten all of my signatures for the Eagle Project Proposal, and have constructed the kit with hopes to install the project by Sep. 2025.

Letters of Recomendation

These are letters to 4 people that show that you follow the scout law and oath in your everyday life.

What is Done?

I have sent out emails to 5 teachers, and all of them have accepted. So far 4 of them have sent in the final, and I have the 5th.

Merit Badges

Merit badges are work that you do (kind of like school work) where you have to complete different requirements to recive them

What I have Left

I have completed majority of my merit badges. All I have left are Cooking, Camping, and Sustainability.

The End
